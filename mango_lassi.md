# Mango Lassi

Simple & soothing drink, ideal with spicy dishes.

## Ingredients

For two glasses (~4dl)

- 1 ripe mango
- 1 small pot of yogurt (~150ml?)
- 130 ml of milk

## Steps

1. Cut the mango in small pieces.
2. Blend all the ingredients together (< 1 min).
3. That's it!
